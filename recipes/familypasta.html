Family Pasta

Description

This family pasta recipe has been in the family for a long time.

My brother liked to cook it a lot. There isn't much needed, but it's a meal that fills up pretty good. And its perfect for a group of people.

Ingredients (for 2 people)

  • 200 gr of pasta
  • 300 gr of minced meat
  • 1 paprika
  • 1 onion
  • 2 cloves of garlic
  • Can of tomato puree (70gr)
  • 500 gr of tomato passata
  • Teaspoon of Italian herbs
  • Cheese as garnish

Steps

  1. Cook the pasta according to its package.
  2. Cut up the onion and press the garlic. Put it in a sauce pan with a little bit of olive oil.
  3. When brown, add the minced meat and add salt and herbs to your liking.
  4. Cut up the paprika in tiny bits and add it to the meat and let it cook for a few minutes while turning regularly.
  5. Add the tomato puree, stir the mixture until everything in the pan is covered with the puree.
  6. Let it cook a little, until the puree loses its sweet-ish fragrance and it smells a bit more baked.
  7. Add the passata, stir it as a whole, and add the Italian herbs.
  8. Let it simmer for a few minutes. Serve with the cheese.
