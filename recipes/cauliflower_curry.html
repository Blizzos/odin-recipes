Cauliflower Curry

Description

It's time for another great curry recipe.

This cauliflower curry is ideal for afternoons when you don't feel that much like cooking. This meal is done in about 20 minutes!

Ingredients

  • 250 gr of cauliflower
  • 200 gr of rice
  • 200 gr of chicken filet
  • 1 cm of ginger
  • 2 spoons of red curry paste
  • 200 ml of coconut milk
  • fresh parsley

Steps

  1. Cook the rice according to its packaging. Cook the cauliflower for a short amount of time, about 4 to 5 minutes. Drain the water. Cut the chicken into pieces and bake them in a saucepan with some olive oil for about 5 minutes until they're golden brown.
  2. Add the curry paste and the coconut milk to the chicken.
  3. Peel the ginger and cut it above the pan.
  4. Put the cauliflower through the curry and serve together with the rice.
  5. Add some fresh parsley on top to finish the dish!
